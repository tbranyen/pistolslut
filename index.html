Pistol Slut


by maryrosecook
2D platform shooter in JavaScript

Use Google Chrome. Clear your cache and do a hard refresh.

New: a nice park, multiple weapons for enemies, spotters for mortarers, aimable grenades (hold c), lifts, MAC-10 and SPAS shotgun, enemies can be suppressed.

Follow Pistol Slut on Twitter.
